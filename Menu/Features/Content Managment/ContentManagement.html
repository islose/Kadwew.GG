Frequently Asked Questions

What is Kadwew?

Kadwew is a screen recording and video editing software designed for gamers and content creators. It allows users to capture their gaming sessions, create AI-generated clips, and edit videos effortlessly.

How does the AI clipping feature work?

The AI clipping feature automatically identifies and captures the best moments from your gaming sessions, making it easy to create highlight reels without manual editing.

Is Kadwew free to use?

Kadwew offers a free version with basic features, while advanced features are available through a subscription plan.

Can I use Kadwew with my favorite games?

Yes, Kadwew is compatible with a wide range of games and platforms, including PC, console, and streaming services.

How do I get started with Kadwew?

To get started, simply download the Kadwew software from our website, install it, and create an account. Once you're set up, you can start recording and editing your gaming sessions.

What are the system requirements for Kadwew?

Kadwew requires a modern PC with a compatible graphics card and sufficient storage space. For detailed system requirements, please refer to our website.

Can I share my videos directly from Kadwew?

Yes, Kadwew allows you to share your edited videos directly to popular social media platforms and streaming services.

Is there a mobile version of Kadwew?

Currently, Kadwew is available only for PC. However, we are exploring options for mobile compatibility in the future.

How can I contact Kadwew support?

If you have any questions or need assistance, you can reach out to our support team through the contact form on our website.

Are there any tutorials available for using Kadwew?

Yes, we provide a variety of tutorials and guides on our website to help you get the most out of Kadwew's features.
